# Split AI Roundup Into Focused Series

## Why
The existing public AI engineering roundup is too broad. Each technical direction needs its own article so readers can evaluate the design space, implementation contracts, risks, and open-source routes without mixing unrelated topics.

## What Changes
- Remove the single broad `OpenSource-AI-Engineering-Roundup` post.
- Add focused public posts for Adaptive RAG, speech LLM alignment, ASR data quality, PEFT, inference serving, ASR-noise structured extraction, and agentic engineering.
- Update generated-site checks from 9 to 15 posts and scan the new series for disclosure markers.

## Non-Goals
- Do not publish private project handoff notes, raw workspace context, business logic, internal ideas, local paths, host details, or company/customer scenario details.
- Do not republish the removed LMDeploy duplex implementation article or its token-decision logic.
- Do not modify the local private review blog content.
